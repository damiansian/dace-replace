🚀 Deliver Badge 📋 Week 13 · Apr 7 🔧 Workshop

Weeks 11 and 12 were dense — six lessons and four capstone components across two weeks. This week is intentionally different. There is no new lesson content. This is working time with structure.

Use this week to finish the ongoing threads, strengthen anything from the first two weeks that needs more depth, and begin thinking about your portfolio as a whole.

🎯 Three Priorities This Week

1. Complete the Ongoing Threads

If you have not yet completed Components 2 and 6, this is the week to close them out.

Component 2: PEAS Session Reflection

If you have attended your PEAS session, write the reflection now. If you have not attended one yet, this is urgent — schedule or watch a recorded session this week. The reflection must document what happened, connect at least one finding to your product using SBI or COIN, and recommend a next step.

Component 6: Stakeholder Connections

If you have not yet introduced yourself to your Legal and PM counterparts, do it this week. Document who they are, what their role covers, and one thing you learned about how accessibility decisions flow through your organization.

2. Strengthen Your Week 11-12 Components

Review Components 1, 3, 4, and 5 against the rubric criteria. Ask yourself: Is this specific to my product, or could it describe any product? Is the evidence grounded in data? Would someone outside my team understand what I am recommending and why? Use this week to fill gaps and add depth.

3. Start Thinking About Portfolio Format

Your final portfolio ties all six components into a coherent story. Start thinking about the format that works best for you:

  • A slide deck with narrative
  • A written report or document
  • A narrative website (AI-assisted tools encouraged)
  • A video walkthrough
  • A combined format
