Week 13 Applied Skills: Workshop + Catch-Up

Dedicated time to strengthen your work, finish ongoing threads, and begin assembling your portfolio.

🚀 Deliver Badge 📋 Week 13 · Apr 7 🔧 Workshop & Working Time

Weeks 11 and 12 were dense — three lessons each, two new capstone components each. This week is the pressure relief valve. No new components. No new content to absorb. Just time to do the work well.

Use this session for whatever you need most: strengthening components that feel thin, finishing the ongoing threads you have not gotten to yet, or starting to see how everything fits together as a portfolio.

🎯 Where to Focus Your Time

Not everyone needs the same thing this week. Use this checklist to figure out where you are and what to prioritize.

If your Week 11 or 12 submissions feel thin:

Go back and strengthen them. Add more data to your problem definition (C1). Make your business case more specific to your stakeholders (C4). Sharpen your recommendation so someone could act on it (C5). Revisit your contribution artifact and make sure it is genuinely tailored to your team (C3). These are all still working drafts — this is the time to improve them.

If you have not done your PEAS session yet (C2):

This is the week to make it happen. Attend, observe, or watch a recorded session and write your reflection. You have through Week 14, but getting it done now frees up your final weeks for portfolio assembly.

If you have not made your stakeholder connections yet (C6):

Reach out to your Legal and PM counterparts this week. You now have enough context from the legal frameworks and business case lessons to have a meaningful conversation. Document who they are and what you learned.

If you are in good shape on all components:

Start thinking about your final portfolio format. How will you tell the story? What ties the components together? Begin assembling — even a rough structure helps you see what is strong and what needs more work.
