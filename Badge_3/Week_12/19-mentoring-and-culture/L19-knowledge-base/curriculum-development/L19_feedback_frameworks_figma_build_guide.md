### Frame 1.2: Different learners need different support

**Type:** Three-column

**Headline:** Calibrate the mentoring approach

**Content:**
- Discover Badge: fundamentals and pattern recognition
- Define Badge: AT behavior and validation reasoning
- Untrained designer: concrete language, minimal jargon, visible consequences

**Speaker notes:**
Mentoring quality depends on matching the explanation to the learner's current level.

---

### Frame 1.3: Mentoring moment vs review moment

**Type:** Two-column

**Headline:** Build understanding vs evaluate the work

**Left column:**
- Heading: `Mentoring moment`
- Explain concepts
- Build judgment
- Slow down for learning

**Right column:**
- Heading: `Review moment`
- Evaluate current work
- Clarify action
- Keep the decision visible

**Speaker notes:**
Both matter. Confusing them weakens either the teaching or the review.
