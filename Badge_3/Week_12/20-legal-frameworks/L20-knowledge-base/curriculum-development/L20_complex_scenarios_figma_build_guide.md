### Frame 1.2: Two core frameworks

**Type:** Two-column

**Headline:** US federal context and European harmonized context

**Left column:**
- Heading: `Section 508`
- US federal procurement
- Relevant to products sold to federal agencies
- Important for compliance and sales context

**Right column:**
- Heading: `EN 301 549`
- European harmonized ICT accessibility standard
- Frequently discussed alongside WCAG conformance
- Important for European obligations

**Speaker notes:**
Both frameworks matter, but they matter in different procurement and market contexts.

### Frame 3.2: Floor vs ceiling

**Type:** Two-column

**Headline:** Compliance is the floor, not the ceiling

**Left column:**
- Heading: `Legal risk`

### Frame 3.3: Communicating without fear

**Type:** Summary

**Headline:** Avoid compliance theater

**Bullet list:**
- Be precise about what is legally required
- Be clear about what is recommended beyond compliance
- Keep user impact visible alongside legal risk
