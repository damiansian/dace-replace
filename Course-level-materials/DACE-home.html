🏅 Your Certification Journey

Progress through three badges to earn your DACE credential.

🔍

Discover Badge

Testing Foundation | Weeks 1 to 5

Build your foundation by learning to test designs against the Adobe Design Accessibility Checklist and create blueline annotations for engineering handoff.

Accessible Names Content Structure Color Forms Keyboard
🎧

Define Badge

Assistive Technology Expertise | Weeks 6 to 10

Understand why accessibility issues matter by learning how assistive technology actually works. Model what a screen reader will announce by examining a design.

DOM & A11y Tree Screen Readers Voice Control High Contrast
🌟

Deliver Badge

Independent Review & Leadership | Weeks 11 to 15

Lead accessibility reviews, mentor other designers, and advocate for accessibility in product strategy. Become a go-to accessibility resource for your team.

Review Methodology Feedback & Crits Leadership Legal

📖 Discover Badge Lessons

Access individual lesson pages for the Discover Badge (Weeks 1 to 5).

Discover Badge curriculum: 8 lessons covering accessibility testing fundamentals
Lesson Topic Week
L01 Accessible Names: WCAG 1.1.1 and 4.1.2, ARIA labels, alt text Week 1
L02 Content Structure: Heading hierarchy, nesting, and structure Week 1
L03 Color: Color contrast, color as information, color vision simulation Week 2
L04 Forms & Errors: Form field labeling, error handling, validation Week 2
L05 Keyboard & Touch: Keyboard navigation, touch targets, focus management, and reading order Week 3
L06 Reflow & Zoom: Content reflow, 400% zoom, responsive design Week 4
L07 Landmarks & Navigation: Skip links, landmarks, wayfinding Week 4
L08 Motion: Animation limits, reduced motion preferences Week 4

🎯 Week 5: Discover Badge Exam

Cumulative assessment covering all Discover Badge lessons (L01-L08).

Assessment Details

  • Format: 25 scenario-based multiple choice questions
  • Time: Untimed, open book
  • Attempts: 1 attempt only
  • Coverage: Accessible names, headings, color, forms, keyboard, reflow, landmarks, and motion
  • Scoring: Raw score appears immediately; instructor reviews and posts final score within 24 hours

Hardware

  • Mac computer with VoiceOver
  • Windows access for NVDA and High Contrast Mode
  • iOS device (recommended for mobile testing)
