🔍 Discover Badge 📅 Week 1 💬 Discussion

Welcome to DACE Cohort 1! Before we dive into accessibility testing, let's take a moment to get to know each other. You'll be learning alongside your cohort members for the next 15 weeks, sharing insights, asking questions, and supporting each other's growth.

Head to the discussion board and introduce yourself to the group. There's no right or wrong way to do this: share what feels comfortable and helps others get to know you.

✏️ What to Share

Consider including some or all of the following in your introduction:

  • Your name and role at Adobe: What do you do day-to-day?
  • Your team: Which product, org, or group are you part of?
  • Why you joined DACE: What drew you to this program? What are you hoping to learn or accomplish?
  • Your accessibility experience: Have you worked on accessibility before? Brand new to it? Somewhere in between? All levels are welcome here.
  • A fun fact about yourself: Hobbies, interests, pets, favorite snacks: anything that helps us get to know the person behind the screen.

🤝 Community Covenant

This program is built on a culture of grace. We acknowledge that we are all learning. We offer each other grace to make mistakes, recognizing that errors are a necessary part of growth.

We correct the work, not shame the designer. You are safe to say "I don't know" or "I messed up."

💡 Tips

  • Reply to others: After posting your introduction, take a few minutes to welcome other cohort members. A simple "Great to meet you!" goes a long way.
  • Keep it casual: This is a low-pressure way to break the ice. No need for formality.
  • Check back later: New cohort members may post throughout the week, so drop by again to say hello.
