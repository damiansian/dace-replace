Complete the following steps for this self-paced activity.

1

Open the Figma Slides

🎨 Figma Slides: Headings →
2

Submit Your Reflection

After reviewing the slides, share your thoughts on what you learned. Consider the following prompts:

  • Aha moments: What concepts clicked or surprised you?
  • Questions: What are you still curious about or need clarification on?
  • Concerns: Are there any aspects you find challenging or confusing?
  • Comments: Any other thoughts or observations?
  • Accolades: What did you find particularly helpful or well-explained?

Your reflection helps us understand your learning journey and improve the course materials. There are no wrong answers here!

📝 Submission

Use the submission area below to share your reflection.

Tip: Your reflection should be at least 2 to 3 sentences. The more detail you share, the better we can support your learning!
